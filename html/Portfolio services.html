3D Modeling and Sculpting
3D modelling is the process of creating three-dimensional representations of an object or a surface. 3D models are made within computer-based 3D modelling software.
During the 3D modelling process, you can determine an object's size, shape, and texture. While 3D sculpting is better for creating organic and freeform designs. The choice between
3D modeling vs sculpting depends on the project's requirements, the artist's skill level, and the available software and hardware.
